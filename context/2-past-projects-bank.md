# [Company Name] — Projects & Achievements Log

This file contains granular project details, achievements, and supplementary context
for your time at [Company Name]. Add entries chronologically. The skill will reference
this file to surface specific results that may not appear in your master CV.

---

## Format

| Date | Category | Achievement / Business Impact |
|------|----------|-------------------------------|
| [Month Year] | [Category] | [What you did and what it resulted in. Include metrics where possible.] |

---

## Categories to use
- Process Improvement
- Testing / Strategy
- Segmentation / Audience
- Automation / Tooling
- Onboarding / Training
- Reporting / Analytics
- Awards / Nominations
- Learning / Certifications
- Stakeholder Management

---

## Example entries

| Date | Category | Achievement / Business Impact |
|------|----------|-------------------------------|
| January 2023 | Process Improvement | Redesigned QA workflow for [campaign type], reducing review time by [X hours] per launch and cutting error rates significantly. |
| March 2023 | Testing / Strategy | Executed A/B test for [merchant/partner] incorporating [variable]; results showed [X%] lift in [metric]. Presented findings to [audience]. |
| September 2023 | Segmentation / Audience | Led segmentation update project using [tool], creating [X] new audience segments. A/B test results showed [X%] uplift in [metric] vs. control. Presented at [internal event]. |
| September 2023 | Awards / Nominations | Nominated for [Award Name] at [company event] by [nominator]. |

---

## Notes for AI use

When tailoring a CV, check this file for:
- Specific metrics not captured in the master CV
- Project names and outcomes that can be woven into bullet points
- Awards and recognitions worth surfacing for senior roles
- Evidence of leadership, training, or cross-functional impact
